OUR STORY

Stepping into Oishii you will feel you have been trasnported to Japan. Sitting at our tables you will enjoy a traditional and elegant handcrafted meal currated by our brilliant chef and his staff. Our menu is carefully created so that everyone can find their own little taste of Japan. We have a large variety of noodles, ranging from ramen to udon. Our large sushi menu contains fresh nigiri, sashimi, and outstanding makizushi.
